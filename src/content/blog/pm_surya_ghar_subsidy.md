## The Eligibility Criteria (Simplified)

There are three non-negotiable rules for the residential subsidy:

1.  **Residential Connection:** Your meter must be "Residential". Commercial, Industrial, or Agricultural meters do not qualify for *this specific* scheme (Farmers have PM-KUSUM instead).
2.  **Ownership:** You must own the roof rights. If you live in an apartment, you need roof rights or a Common Area connection. Tenants cannot apply directly.
3.  **Active Connection:** You must have an active consumer number with MSEDCL (Mahavitaran).
